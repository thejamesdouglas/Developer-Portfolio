About Me

James Douglas Ivancic is a songwriter based in University Heights, Ohio. Hailing from Euclid and Madison, OH, JD got his start as the singer/guitarist of The Pitted Moonshine Cherries, runner-up of the 2002 Mountain Dew High School Rock Off (if you know the current location of Johnny Psycho & The Switchblade Rockers, we'll pay you a bounty) and the short-lived 4-piece Canterbury Arts Club. The official release of SUMA SESSIONS 2002, an EP recorded at the legendary Suma Recording Studio in August of '02, will be officially released, in full, 9.20.22. Its' first single LOVE IS SUICIDE saw its' release on 9.6.22 after 20 years of existing solely on CDR's burned in JD's dorm room while skipping very expensive classes sold to him by the ruling elite. His 2nd solo record is in production and will come out eventually if you don't die first.
